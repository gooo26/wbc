Luxury Travel Agency Landing Page Template 

A premium landing page template for modern luxury travel agencies to showcase curated destinations, memberships, and travel journals with immersive visuals.

Voyage is a luxury travel agency landing page template designed for brands that curate high-end, immersive journeys.

What's Included
Hero with bold headline, social proof, and dual CTAs
Destination bento grid for featured trips and pricing
Value proposition section highlighting key service pillars
Visual spotlight banner, moodboards, and editorial-style journal
Newsletter capture and full modern footer with links and social icons
Key Features
Dark, glassmorphism-inspired aesthetic with large type
Curated destination cards with pricing and tags
Membership and concierge storytelling blocks
Editorial journal layout for content marketing
Responsive Tailwind CSS layout ready to customize
Primary Use Case
Ideal for luxury travel agencies and boutique tour operators needing a high-impact, brand-forward landing page template to promote curated itineraries, memberships, and travel content.
