Luxury Watch Product Landing Page Template

A high-end product landing page template featuring a 3D
 hero experience, specs, technology highlights, and ecommerce CTAs for premium watches.

CHRONOS OBSIDIAN is a dark, futuristic product landing page template built for 
premium watches and wearables, combining a cinematic 3D hero with conversion-focused 
ecommerce sections.

Included Sections

Immersive 3D interactive hero with scroll-driven animations
Specs and materials panel in glassmorphism style
Technology highlight section with bold typography
Feature grid, pricing, and add-to-cart CTA
Sticky navigation and minimalist footer links

Key Features

Three.js + GSAP-powered 3D product showcase
Dark, minimal, glass-panel aesthetic for luxury brands
Clear PRE-ORDER / ADD TO CART purchase flows
Responsive layout optimized for desktop and mobile
Smooth scroll and micro-interactions that feel premium

Primary Use Case: Perfect template for launching or showcasing a high-end watch, 
smartwatch, or advanced wearable with a focus on engineering, technology, and brand storytelling.
