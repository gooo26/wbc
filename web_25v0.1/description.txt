Contractor AI Voice Agent Landing Page Template


A high-converting landing page template for promoting AI voice agents that answer, qualify, and
schedule calls for contractors and home service businesses.

Lead Fix AI is a dark, futuristic landing page template built to market AI voice agents for
contractors and home service providers.

What's Included

Hero with bold value proposition, demo phone mockup, and dual CTAs
“How It Works” service cards for answering, qualifying, and scheduling
Feature grid introducing the AI assistant (Lisa) and capabilities
Testimonial and metrics block to showcase social proof
Tiered pricing plans for SaaS or service offerings
Contact and discovery call form with qualification fields

Key Features

3D animated background using Three.js and GSAP
Contractor-focused messaging for roofers, HVAC, plumbers
Conversion-optimized CTAs like “Book a Discovery Call” and “Get Started”
Pricing comparison layout for Starter, Professional, Premium plans
Responsive, Tailwind-powered design with dark, gradient styling


Primary Use Case: A reusable template for agencies, SaaS founders, 
or service providers selling AI voice agent solutions to contractors and local trade businesses.
