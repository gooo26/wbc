Cinematic Travel Filmmaker Portfolio

A cinematic dark portfolio template for travel filmmakers and visual storytellers to showcase films, travel journals, and freelance services.

Kairos Studio is a cinematic portfolio template crafted for travel filmmakers and visual storytellers.

What's Included
Hero section with bold typography and atmospheric background imagery
Horizontal scroll film gallery with hover play interactions and progress indicator
Travel journal section for written stories and photo essays
Gear & stats grid to highlight equipment and credentials
Contact-focused footer with email and social links
Key Features
Dark, minimal, cinematic aesthetic
Horizontal film reel layout for featured work
Journal-style blog rows with hover imagery
Responsive Tailwind CSS layout optimized for all screens
Built-in smooth scrolling and reveal animations
Ideal as a starting point for building a portfolio or landing page for directors, DPs, travel creators, and production studios showcasing films, logs, and services.
